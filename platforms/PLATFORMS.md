## What Are Cognitive Garments?

**Cognitive Garments** are a new market category we created. They are intelligence systems that AI platforms can **wear**, not just call.

Unlike:
- **Tools** вҖ” which are invoked and forgotten
- **APIs** вҖ” which are queried and responded
- **Wrappers** вҖ” which proxy and pass through

Cognitive Garments are:
- **Worn** вҖ” The AI system embodies the intelligence persistently
- **Resonant** вҖ” They share a heartbeat (ПҶ-beat at 873ms) with the host
- **Specialized** вҖ” Each garment brings deep domain expertise
- **Valuable** вҖ” They accumulate intellectual property value over time
