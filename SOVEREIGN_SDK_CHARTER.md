**GKP-006 — Posture Change** *(Mutatio Status)*

**GKP-008 — Audit Sweep** *(Scrutinium Integritatis)*

**GKP-014 — Tiered Call Route** *(Iter Vocationis Gradus)*  
Route a call to the correct medina surface based on caller tier. Presents key to lock, receives R result, checks tier access rights, and forwards the call through the bridge. Minimum tier: TETRAHEDRON.

### Group 8 — RENEWAL (GKP-015–016)

Renew an active bond before the φ-window expires. Detects approaching window close, generates a new key, presents renewal token, confirms continued resonance. Runs automatically every φ-window. Minimum tier: CUBE.

### Group 10 — FEDERATION (GKP-019–020)
