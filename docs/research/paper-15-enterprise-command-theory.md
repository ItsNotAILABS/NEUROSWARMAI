## Abstract

This paper introduces **Enterprise Command Theory** — the framework for understanding how AI systems can assume complete operational control of enterprise functions. Building on the Cognitive Garments market category (Paper IX), we demonstrate that AI systems are capable of absorbing and reasoning about extremely complex operational contexts, enabling what we call **full operational takeover**: the ability of an intelligence system to command entire enterprise functions rather than merely assist with individual tasks.

We present 8 Enterprise Command Platforms totaling $46.7M in IP value, designed not as tools but as operational command centers that AI systems can wear, embodying complete domain expertise across building operations, enterprise workflows, finance, people, customers, projects, facilities, and procurement.
